Harvest Organics

Developed a nature-inspired brand system and packaging that strengthens shelf presence, authenticity, and consumer trust.

Services:
Packaging Design + Brand System
Industry:
Food & Sustainable Goods
Timeline
6 Weeks
View live site

The Challenge

Harvest Organics wanted packaging and a brand system that communicated freshness and sustainability, but their visuals felt generic and failed to stand out on shelves or digital channels.

  • Weak brand differentiation
  • Inconsistent product labeling
  • Limited storytelling around sourcing
  • Low shelf impact

The Solution

We built a nature-inspired identity using warm tones, organic patterns, and clean typography. Packaging layouts were standardized, and storytelling elements were added to highlight quality and sustainability.

The Result

The refreshed brand gained stronger recognition both online and in retail environments, driving higher customer trust and improved sales performance.

  • Increase in product visibility
  • Stronger customer recall
  • Higher sales lift across key items

The new brand tells our story instantly. Customers connect with us more, and the packaging stands out everywhere.
